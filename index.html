Minecraft is an electronic game of the sandbox and survival genres that does not have specific objectives to be achieved, allowing players great freedom of choice on how to play it. However, there is an achievement system, known as "achievements" in the Java edition.



Your goal is to collect resources (wood, rocks, ores, coal, food) and build ever better tools and shelters to survive and save yourself from the monsters that come out at night. But it's not just about survival that the game is about. Minecraft is also about exploration and constant evolution.
