As the name implies, Minecraft is a game where the main mechanics are mining and crafting items and tools. With the right materials, such as wood, iron and stone, you can create building elements, weapons and armor.

However, you need to combine the raw materials. A good tip is to consult the site minecraftcrafting.info, which has detailed guides on how to create each type of item.
